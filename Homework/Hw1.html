Credo School

Credo School aims to provide a safe and productive environment within which learning can take place at a fast pace. We believe that all children, irrespective of their background, are entitled to achieve quality education that caters to the needs of time in which they are growing up, along with also endowing them with excellent knowledge of the disciplines they have chosen. A rigorous educational program is in place at Credo which encourages students to think deeply and independently.


We welcome prospective students to experience a devoted faculty, strong management and an interactive learning environment.
